Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


1. How do you recommend anime?

We use a combination of algorithms, user ratings, and genre-based filters to recommend anime titles. You can also customize your preferences by selecting specific genres, themes, or studios you like.


2. Do I need to create an account?

While you can browse general recommendations without an account, creating one allows you to receive personalized suggestions, track your watched list, and participate in community discussions.


3. Is OtakuGuide free to use?

Yes, our website is free to use. However, we may offer premium features or ad-free options in the future.


4. Can I watch anime directly on your website?

No, OtakuGuide is an anime recommendation and discovery platform. We do not host or stream any content directly. However, we provide links to legal streaming platforms where available.
